Why Everyone is Playing

Pickleball is the fastest-growing sport in America. It combines elements of tennis, badminton, and ping-pong, making it incredibly accessible for beginners but highly competitive for advanced players.

The Basics to Get Started

  1. Essential Equipment
    • Pickleball Paddle (Composite or Graphite)
    • Plastic perforated balls (Indoor vs. Outdoor)
    • Court shoes for lateral movement
  2. Core Rules to Remember
    • Stay out of "The Kitchen" (Non-Volley Zone)
    • The Double Bounce Rule on the serve
    • Games are usually played to 11 (win by 2)
